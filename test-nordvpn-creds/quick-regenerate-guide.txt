QUICK GUIDE: Regenerate NordVPN Service Credentials
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Go to: https://my.nordaccount.com/dashboard/nordvpn/

2. Look for "Service Credentials" section (NOT your login credentials)

3. Click "Generate New Service Credentials" or "Regenerate"

4. Copy the NEW username and password (24 chars each)

5. Update .env file:
   NORDVPN_USERNAME=<paste new username>
   NORDVPN_PASSWORD=<paste new password>
   
   (No quotes, no spaces around =)

6. Test: cd test-nordvpn-creds && ./test.sh
